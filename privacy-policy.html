Information We Collect

Section 02

We collect information in three ways: data you provide manually, data generated automatically from your device, and data collected by third-party SDKs we integrate (detailed in Section 8).

2.1 Data You Provide

  • Video URLs — links you paste or browse to analyze and download
  • Search queries — search terms entered in the built-in browser
  • Custom configurations — custom ad block list URLs and file renaming strings

2.2 Automatically Collected Data

The following are collected automatically when you use certain features:

  • App settings — chosen language locale, default video resolution, and adblock configurations stored locally
  • Download and browser history — bookmark lists, search suggestions, and download queues stored locally (SQLite Database)
  • Purchase information — billing receipts processed securely via Google Play Billing
  • Advertising identifiers — collected by Google AdMob when ads are shown — see Section 5
  • App usage events — collected by Firebase Analytics — see Section 6
  • Device information — model, OS version, language — collected by Firebase and AdMob

2.3 Data NOT Collected by Us

LogicGridApps does not require your name, email, phone number, or social sign-in credentials for core App use. We do not transmit your downloaded video files to external servers. All downloads are saved directly on your local device.

Network & Storage Access

Section 03
Storage access disclosure: This App needs access to your local storage to save downloaded video/audio files and display them in the "Downloaded" section. We only read and write files inside the user's specific storage paths.

What We Access

Local storage (Download folder) Internet connections (Web browser) Private vault files

How This Access is Used

  • Internet permission is used to load web pages, analyze streaming links, and perform the download
  • Storage permissions are used to write the downloaded files and display existing local files inside the gallery

Revoke permissions anytime: Settings → Apps → Video Downloader → Permissions.

Video Downloader Engine

Section 04

When you browse or request a download, our built-in engines (such as the Custom Downloader or Youtube-dl wrapper) process the streaming URLs locally or using transient network connections.

Key facts: All downloads are executed directly on your device. We do not cache, inspect, or log your media downloads on private servers.

Advertising — Google AdMob

Section 05
AdMob Disclosure: This App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use data from your device to serve personalized or non-personalized ads. This section explains how advertising works in the App.

Ad Formats Used

⚡ Native / Banner Ads Embedded visual banners shown during search, browser configuration, or downloaded video list screens.
🎬 Rewarded / Interstitial Ads Full-screen visual ads presented during specific download transitions. Ad frequency can be controlled dynamically via Firebase Remote Config.

Data Collected by AdMob

Google AdMob independently collects the following data for ad serving and measurement purposes:

  • Advertising identifier (Google Advertising ID / GAID)
  • Device information (model, OS version, screen size, language)
  • IP address (used to infer approximate geographic location)
  • App interaction signals (ad impressions, clicks)

This data is collected and processed by Google LLC under their own privacy policy. LogicGridApps does not have access to or control over AdMob's data collection.

Ad Personalization

By default, AdMob may serve personalized ads. You can opt out of personalized ads at any time:

  • On your Android device: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization
  • Or reset your Advertising ID: Settings → Google → Ads → Reset Advertising ID

For Google's data practices related to advertising, see: Google Privacy Policy and AdMob Privacy FAQ.

Analytics & Remote Configuration

Section 06

Firebase Analytics

We use Google Firebase Analytics to understand how users interact with the App. This helps us improve features and fix issues. Firebase Analytics automatically collects:

  • Screen views and navigation events
  • App open/close and session duration
  • Device type, OS version, app version, language
  • Crash and error reports (Firebase Crashlytics)

No personally identifiable data (such as your search queries or vault files) is ever sent to Firebase Analytics.

Firebase Remote Config

We use Firebase Remote Config to dynamically control app behavior without requiring an update — including ad frequency limits, feature flags, and UI layout controls. No personal data is transmitted to Remote Config.
